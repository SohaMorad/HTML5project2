       Food Delivery

We will pick up your food order within 20 minutes of order placement and deliver it immediately.
We do not “stack” orders from different restaurants, thus delaying the delivery time: we deliver
from your restaurant to your customer immediately.

We are open for business from Monday to Friday from 8am to 10pm and at weekends from 4pm to 10pm.

Our couriers are uniformed and equipped with bright orange food delivery boxes. The boxes are firm-sided, flat-bottomed and well insulated, thus allowing meals to be kept hot, cold or frozen for a long period of time. Each box contains adjustable rigid dividers to separate foods and provide shelving for stacking purposes.
