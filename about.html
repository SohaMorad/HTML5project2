Our Story



The Beginning

TurnAround Couriers started as a package delivery business in Toronto in 2002. We started with just four couriers, one receptionist,
one dispatcher and one very clear social mission: to only hire youth who faced barriers to the labour market to work for us as couriers and back office
staff and to do so as a for-profit business. In this way, we would help these youth to “turn around” their lives.

High Profile Clients

Our service levels and social purpose resonated with many high profile businesses in Toronto and we remain proud to have built strong
relationships with many top firms, including Royal Bank of Canada and PriceWaterhouseCoopers. Firms with strong values at their core.
Every day these firms know that the work that they give us, provides work to youth in the city who are looking to turn their lives around.

Education

In 2012 we started to help our couriers to continue their education. George Brown College has helped us hugely in this regard.
TurnAround Couriers helps couriers to pay for their courses while they work at the company.

Recognition

TurnAround Couriers is recognized as one of the largest bike courier firms in Toronto and one of the most successful social enterprises
in Canada.
